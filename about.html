Religio



Province Management Software - "Congregation Sans Paper"

INTRODUCTION

This is a user-friendly software tool for the religious head of a religious institute, province secretary, institute/community heads and for all province members - to manage all communications (circular, email, meetings), create records (chronicles, concerns), maintain member critical and personal information, assignments, history, institution/community information, planning, reporting and archiving

USER BENEFITS

  • Single source for province, member, institution and communities’ details

  • Maintain Member Profile (Education, Sacraments, Profession, Formation, Holy Order etc…)

  • Communication through email to members and communities and institution heads.

  • Sending Greetings on Birthdays & on all special Anniversaries days such as Ordination, feast days.

  • Invite members for meetings

  • Transfer can be done in a single click

  • Various Reports can be taken

  • Publishing Calendar Events & Circular’s and sending them through our application

    • USER GROUP

  • ReligiO addresses five segments of major users: Generalate, Province, Communities, Institution Heads, Members
